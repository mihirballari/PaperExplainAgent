Abstract

PaperExplainAgent is an interactive reading assistant for STEM textbooks, lecture notes, and research papers. Load a PDF, highlight a definition, theorem, figure, or paragraph, and ask questions in plain language. The system responds with layered explanations—starting from high-level intuition and concrete examples, then moving into more technical detail as needed—while always staying grounded in the original passage you selected.

PaperExplainAgent is designed to make dense material in mathematics, computer science, physics, engineering, and related fields easier to digest. It can unpack symbols and notation, clarify assumptions, compare similar concepts, and outline the structure of an argument or derivation step by step. As the project develops, we plan to add richer workflows such as saving explanation “threads,” generating study notes, and integrating with common reference managers and note-taking tools.
